Woven through our latest colelction of fabrics is a story of color and form that dates back to the Bauhaus school—a place and method of teaching in pre-War Germany that intersected beauty with function, mass production with artistic vision, and a variety of disciplines from art to architecture

Our winter collections bear the mark of the Bauhaus. We took inspiration from the artists who taught and studies there, and from the school’s hallmark ideas on color and form, shape and silhouette.
